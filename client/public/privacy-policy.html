4. How long we keep your data

We retain your account and guest-list data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (plans, snapshots, and share links) is deleted immediately. We intend to delete accounts that have been inactive for 2 years, with at least 30 days' prior notice by email where contact is possible.

5. Who we share data with (Processors)

We do not sell your data. We use the following processors strictly to run the service:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication (stores your account and guest-list data).
  • Fly.io — application hosting (runs the web application).

These providers process data on our instructions under data-processing agreements. Where data is transferred outside the UK, appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) apply.

6. Cookies and local storage

Tableaux does not use tracking or advertising cookies. To keep you signed in and to back up your in-progress work, we store strictly necessary data in your browser's local storage (your authentication session and an autosave copy of your plan). These are essential to the service and, under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), do not require consent. Clearing your browser storage will sign you out.

  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
